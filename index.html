#Edinburgh University Salsa Society

The Salsa Society is a multicultural dance society that brings Latin warmth and energy to Edinburgh.
Salsa is a popular form of social dance that originated in the Caribbean. The movements of salsa have origins in Cuban Son, Cha-cha-cha, Mambo and Puertorican Bomba & Plena and other dance forms, and the dance, along with the salsa music, originated in the mid-1970s in New York
